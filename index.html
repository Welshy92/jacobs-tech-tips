Windows Simplified:

Improving reliability & efficiency

Make your life easier

Using a computer is an essential part of most peoples days. Whether it's used for work, browsing, socialising or playing, they all require some skill to be able to use them. The problem is that a lot of the basics are either glossed over or not taught at all leading to a slower and more frustrating experience for yourself while using computers. Here at Windows Simplified we aim to teach some of those basic skills that both you and your computer will be thankful for. There are a few key areas that it has been split up into for you.

  • Keyboard shortcuts, while something that is not required, is something that will allow you to perform tasks a bit faster. There are shortcuts for a wide variety of things. Head to the Keyboard Shortcuts page to find out more.
  • The second area of focus is all to do with maintaining your computer. System maintenance is essential to keeping your computers running smooth and to keep using the most up to date software.
  • The final area to look at is simply a list of useful websites and software that will aid you with various computing functions. These range from browsers, to email apps, to software that will help your PC run smoothly.
